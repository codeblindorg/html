  • Orphaning

I was born and raised for the most part in a small town in North Carolina called Princeton. It was a great place for the most part but at age 11 I was orphaned. My mother was murdered by the man she was going to for treatment addiction. A Psychologist by the name of Robert Brewington. A man I will hate with all of my soul until the day I take my last breath.

My mother struggled with drugs and addiction for many years and was overcoming it. She was going to AA, NA, and a few others and THEY referred her to this psycho. He raped my mother as well as a few other women coming to him for treatment. When she was about to go forward and press charges he started making death threats and when that didn't work he had my mother killed.

The Police "worked" on that case for maybe a week and then decided not to anymore. The justice system failed me and my sister, and my mother. She died for nothing. Just like so many other nameless numbers in their file cabinet. I realized back then that NO cop was a good cop and my dislike for the entire Law Enforcement process and system only grew
